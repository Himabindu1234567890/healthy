About NutriSense

AI-Powered Healthy Eating & Nutrition Risk Analysis Platform

What is NutriSense?

NutriSense is a data-driven health analytics platform designed to analyze daily food intake, predict nutrition risk levels, and assist users in making healthier dietary decisions. The system combines nutrition data, machine learning, and interactive dashboards to provide meaningful health insights.

Problem Statement

Poor dietary habits are a major contributor to chronic diseases such as diabetes, obesity, and cardiovascular disorders. Many individuals lack awareness of their daily nutritional intake and its long-term health impact. NutriSense addresses this gap by providing an intelligent, explainable, and user-friendly nutrition risk assessment system.

How NutriSense Works

🥗 Food & Quantity Input
📊 Nutrient Calculation
🧠 ML Risk Prediction
📈 Interactive Dashboard
